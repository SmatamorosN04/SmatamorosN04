# 💫 About Me

Hi there! My name is **Sergio Armando Matamoros Nuñez**, and I am a Computer Engineering student and Junior Full Stack Developer. I have a strong passion for software development, artificial intelligence, and educational applications. What excites me the most about technology is its potential to transform the way we learn, communicate, and solve real-world problems.

During my academic journey, I have worked on projects that integrate innovation and learning, such as intelligent chatbots, interactive platforms, and simulations of physical processes. These experiences have allowed me to grow as a communicator, a problem solver, and a leader in collaborative projects.

I see myself as a proactive and adaptable developer who is always eager to learn new technologies and take on new challenges. My long-term goal is to continue developing innovative solutions that combine software engineering, artificial intelligence, and education.
